## üé® Design & Messaging Principles

### 1. CREDIBILITY FIRST
- Always cite certifications (NABL)
- Reference awards and recognitions
- Show scientific partnerships
- Use real performance data

### 2. CLARITY OVER COMPLEXITY
- Avoid jargon unless necessary
- Explain technical terms
- Use analogies for complex concepts
- Lead with benefits, follow with features

### 3. PROOF POINTS
- Specific metrics (50%, not "high")
- Certifications and validations
- Field-test results
- Customer testimonials (when available)

### 4. COMPETITIVE DIFFERENTIATION
- Show why we're different
- Use comparison tables
- Highlight unique advantages
- Explain "why this matters"

### 5. SCALE & IMPACT
- Emphasize commercial readiness
- Show manufacturing capability
- Demonstrate market deployment
- Connect to larger impact (clean energy, water, air)
